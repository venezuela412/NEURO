# NEURO Demo Script

## 60-second version

1. Open the Mini App landing screen.
2. Say: "NEURO turns idle TON into simple income plans inside Telegram."
3. Tap **Start with your plan**.
4. Enter an amount and choose **Earn**.
5. Tap **Generate my plan**.
6. Show the recommendation card and say:
   - "The user does not need to learn DeFi."
   - "NEURO explains the plan in human language, shows the expected range, the safety fallback, and the estimated fee."
7. Tap **Activate this plan** and approve the plan text in the wallet.
8. Show the active plan screen and say:
   - "Now the user sees current mode, estimated value, available to withdraw, and the latest safety-aware update."
9. Tap **View activity**.
10. Say: "Every meaningful change is translated into a calm activity feed instead of raw protocol logs."

## 90-second version

### Opening
- "Most TON holders do nothing with their TON because DeFi is too complex."
- "NEURO fixes that inside Telegram."

### Step 1 — Landing
- Show the landing page.
- Say: "This is not a dashboard. It is a passive income autopilot."
- Point to:
  - Protect
  - Earn
  - Grow
- Say: "These are the only choices most users need."

### Step 2 — Plan generation
- Go to the plan selector.
- Enter `100 TON`.
- Choose **Earn**.
- Optionally switch flexibility and risk.
- Tap **Generate my plan**.
- Say:
  - "Under the hood, NEURO runs deterministic plan logic."
  - "On the surface, the user only sees a simple recommendation."

### Step 3 — Recommendation
- Highlight:
  - estimated monthly range
  - risk level
  - what NEURO will do
  - fallback behavior
  - estimated fee
  - technical details drawer
- Say:
  - "The fee is transparent and only charged on profit."
  - "If conditions deteriorate, NEURO suggests or prepares a safer path."
  - "There is also a hidden technical drawer for judges and advanced users, but the main path stays simple."

### Step 4 — Approval and active plan
- Activate the plan.
- Approve the NEURO plan text in the wallet.
- Show:
  - estimated current value
  - estimated profit
  - estimated NEURO fee
  - available to withdraw
- Say:
  - "This is where the product becomes ongoing, not just transactional."
  - "The user sees a current mode, not protocol clutter."

### Step 5 — Activity
- Open the activity screen.
- Say:
  - "NEURO explains plan events in plain language: prepared, safety checked, monitoring active."

### Close
- "That is the product thesis: put your TON to work simply, safely, and natively inside Telegram."

## What to click

1. `/`
2. **Start with your plan**
3. Enter amount
4. Pick Protect / Earn / Grow
5. **Generate my plan**
6. **Activate this plan**
7. **View activity**

## Backup explanation if something external is flaky

If a wallet or external integration is not available during the demo:

- "The product architecture is ready for TON Connect and adapter-based protocol execution."
- "This build already includes a deterministic plan engine, control-plane-backed preview generation, fee previews, wallet-sign approval, execution state framing, and a technical details drawer."
- "The current implementation prioritizes a polished Mini App flow and a clean non-custodial architecture while keeping live protocol adapters modular."

If Telegram context is unavailable:

- "The app also runs in browser mode for development, but the UX is designed for Telegram Mini App constraints: compact layout, sticky actions, and one-thumb flows."
