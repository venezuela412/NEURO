## Product Hunt style blurb

NEURO is a Telegram-native passive income app for TON. Instead of asking users to understand staking routes, vaults, or LP mechanics, it turns idle TON into a simple plan: Protect, Earn, or Grow. The app explains what will happen, shows estimated outcomes and fees, keeps safety fallback visible, and makes passive income on TON feel calm, clear, and usable for normal people.

## Investor / judge one-paragraph summary

NEURO is a consumer-first TON product that reframes DeFi complexity into simple passive income plans inside Telegram. Users choose Protect, Earn, or Grow; NEURO handles the plan recommendation, transparent fee preview, safety fallback explanation, and portfolio summary in plain language. The architecture combines a polished Mini App, a deterministic plan engine, a clear performance-fee model, and a control-plane seam for persistence and monitoring, making it both hackathon-demo ready and extensible toward a production passive income platform on TON.
